Our Story

The Silversmiths of
Petandakan Village

Petandakan Village, 5 km south of Singaraja, is home to Bali's traditional silver craft — the only industry of its kind in the village. Eva Gold was founded by master silversmith Putu Suda Arta, and has been shaping silver and gold by hand ever since.

Every piece travels from sketch to finished jewel inside the same workshop — cast, set and polished by hand, the conventional process, from beginning to end.

How a Piece Is Made

The Conventional Process, Start to End

We accept orders based on your own design and taste — the entire process takes only 2–3 days.

01

Design

Your stone, your sketch, your taste — the starting point for every piece.

Casting & Forging

Natural stones are set one at a time, entirely by hand.

A final polish brings out the shine before a piece leaves the workshop.
